# Yield Hunter Analytical Reporting Requirements

This document outlines the essential reporting needs of the Yield Hunter revenue optimization system to monitor venue performance, optimize micro-offer deployment, understand guest behavior, and maximize seating utilization and revenue. These reports support multiple teams including revenue management, marketing, operations, and analytics.

---

# 1. Venue Occupancy Overview Report

To improve utilization of dining capacity, this report provides visibility into how venue seating is being used across time periods. It helps identify underutilized windows and supports proactive operational and marketing actions to improve occupancy and revenue.

### Objective

Provide visibility into real-time and historical venue occupancy levels to support capacity optimization and revenue maximization.

### Business Use Cases

This report allows stakeholders to monitor occupancy trends, detect underutilized service periods, and take timely action through operational adjustments or micro-offer deployment.

### Key Data Points

Venue ID, seating capacity, occupied seats, service period, timestamp, walk-in covers, offer-driven covers.

### Metrics & KPIs

Occupancy Rate, Available Seats, Peak vs Off-Peak Utilization, Low Occupancy Instances.

### Interactivity Expectations

Users should be able to drill down by venue, service period, or day of week and view occupancy trends at hourly granularity.

### Key Calculations

Occupancy Rate = Occupied Seats ÷ Total Seats × 100

### Primary Users

Revenue Managers, Operations Managers.

### Security Access

Access to venue-level operational data should be limited to authorized operations and revenue teams.

### Key Business Questions Answered

Which venues are underutilized?
When are peak vs off-peak periods?
Where should offers be deployed?

---

# 2. Micro-Offer Conversion Funnel Report

To improve effectiveness of targeted promotions, this report tracks how micro-offers perform from presentation to redemption.

### Objective

Monitor offer lifecycle performance and conversion efficiency.

### Business Use Cases

Helps identify drop-offs in the offer journey and supports optimization of targeting and discount strategies.

### Key Data Points

Offer ID, presentation timestamp, acceptance timestamp, redemption timestamp, discount tier, party size.

### Metrics & KPIs

Acceptance Rate, Redemption Rate, Expiration Rate.

### Interactivity Expectations

Users should be able to analyze performance by discount tier or venue.

### Key Calculations

Conversion Rate = Redeemed ÷ Presented × 100

### Primary Users

Marketing Teams, Revenue Managers.

### Security Access

Offer-level performance data can be shared across marketing and revenue teams.

### Key Business Questions Answered

Which offers convert best?
Where do drop-offs occur?

---

# 3. Guest Presence Heatmap Report

To improve targeting accuracy, this report visualizes guest movement and dwell behavior across zones.

### Objective

Understand guest distribution and engagement across locations.

### Business Use Cases

Supports location-based targeting of micro-offers.

### Key Data Points

Zone ID, dwell time, presence pings.

### Metrics & KPIs

Unique Guests, Average Dwell Time.

### Interactivity Expectations

Users should be able to view guest density by time and location.

### Key Calculations

Average Dwell Time = Total Dwell Time ÷ Guests

### Primary Users

Operations, Marketing.

### Security Access

Guest identity must be anonymized.

### Key Business Questions Answered

Where are guests spending time?

---

# 4. Revenue Attribution Report

To evaluate financial impact, this report links offer redemptions to revenue generated.

### Objective

Measure revenue contribution of micro-offers.

### Business Use Cases

Supports ROI evaluation.

### Key Data Points

Revenue, discount value, venue.

### Metrics & KPIs

Net Revenue, Revenue per Redemption.

### Interactivity Expectations

Users should compare performance across venues.

### Key Calculations

Net Revenue = Gross – Discount

### Primary Users

Revenue Managers.

### Security Access

Financial data restricted to authorized users.

### Key Business Questions Answered

Are offers profitable?

# 5. Inventory Perishability Report

To reduce lost revenue, this report quantifies unused seating capacity.

### Objective

Track unused capacity risk.

### Business Use Cases

Supports proactive offer deployment.

### Key Data Points

Available seats, time remaining.

### Metrics & KPIs

Unused Capacity Hours, RevPASH.

### Interactivity Expectations

View trends by venue.

### Key Calculations

RevPASH = Revenue ÷ Seat Hours

### Primary Users

Revenue Managers.

### Security Access

Operational access only.

### Key Business Questions Answered

Where is capacity being wasted?
