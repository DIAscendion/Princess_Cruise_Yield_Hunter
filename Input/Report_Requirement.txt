## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### 1.1 Purpose
This document outlines the reporting requirements for the Yield Hunter revenue optimization system. The reports will enable stakeholders to monitor venue occupancy, track micro-offer performance, analyze guest behavior, and optimize revenue generation across dining venues.

### 1.2 Scope
The reporting solution will provide real-time operational dashboards, historical trend analysis, and predictive insights to support data-driven decision making for venue yield management.

### 1.3 Intended Audience
- **Primary Users:** Revenue Managers, Venue Operations Managers, Marketing Teams
- **Secondary Users:** Executive Leadership, Business Intelligence Teams
- **Technical Teams:** Data Engineers, Analytics Teams (for implementation reference)

---

## 2. DATA SOURCES OVERVIEW

### 2.1 Operational Data Sources

**Guest Presence Tracking**
- **Source:** Real-Time Location System (RTLS) / Medallion Readers
- **Update Frequency:** Real-time (every few seconds)
- **Primary Use:** Guest movement tracking, zone dwell time analysis, journey mapping

**Dining Inventory Management**
- **Source:** Property Management System (PMS) / Dining Reservation System
- **Update Frequency:** Every 5-15 minutes
- **Primary Use:** Venue capacity monitoring, occupancy rate tracking, availability management

**Micro-Offer Campaign Events**
- **Source:** Offer Orchestration Service
- **Update Frequency:** Real-time (event-driven)
- **Primary Use:** Offer lifecycle tracking, conversion analysis, revenue attribution

### 2.2 Aggregated Analytics Data

**Venue Performance KPIs**
- **Update Frequency:** Hourly batch processing
- **Primary Use:** Historical trending, performance benchmarking, strategic planning

## 3. REPORTING REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 REPORT: Venue Occupancy Overview

**Business Objective:**  
Monitor real-time and historical occupancy rates across all dining venues to identify low-utilization periods and optimize seating capacity.

**Primary Users:** Venue Operations Managers, Revenue Managers

**Key Metrics:**
- Current occupancy rate (% seats occupied)
- Average available seats by time period
- Low occupancy alerts (occupancy < 45%)
- Peak vs. off-peak occupancy comparison

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Venue name
- Date/time (hourly view)
- Day of week
- Service period (breakfast, lunch, dinner)

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Line chart: Occupancy trend over time
- Heat map: Occupancy by venue and time of day
- KPI cards: Current occupancy, available seats
- Alert indicators: Low occupancy flags (red/yellow/green)

**Refresh Frequency:** Real-time (updates every 5-15 minutes)

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF

---

### 3.2 REPORT: Micro-Offer Conversion Funnel

**Business Objective:**  
Track the performance of micro-offer campaigns from presentation through redemption to measure engagement effectiveness and identify drop-off points.

**Primary Users:** Marketing Teams, Revenue Managers

**Key Metrics:**
- Total offers presented
- Offers accepted (acceptance rate %)
- Offers redeemed (redemption rate %)
- Offers expired (expiration rate %)
- Conversion funnel visualization

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Venue name
- Discount percentage tier
- Party size
- Date range
- Offer type

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Funnel chart: Presented → Accepted → Redeemed
- Bar chart: Conversion rates by discount tier
- Trend line: Daily acceptance/redemption rates
- Table: Detailed offer performance metrics

**Refresh Frequency:** Hourly

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF, PowerPoint

---

### 3.3 REPORT: Guest Presence Heatmap

**Business Objective:**  
Visualize guest movement patterns and zone popularity to optimize venue placement and offer targeting strategies.

**Primary Users:** Operations Managers, Marketing Teams

**Key Metrics:**
- Unique guests per zone
- Average dwell time (minutes)
- Total presence events (pings)
- Average pings per guest (engagement indicator)

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Zone type (casino, pool, theater, etc.)
- Location ID
- Time of day (hourly)
- Day of week

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Heat map: Guest density by zone and time
- Bar chart: Top 10 most-visited zones
- Bubble chart: Dwell time vs. guest count
- Timeline: Guest traffic patterns throughout the day

**Refresh Frequency:** Real-time (updates every 5 minutes)

**Export Options:** PDF, Image

---

### 3.4 REPORT: Revenue Attribution Dashboard

**Business Objective:**  
Measure the incremental revenue generated through micro-offer redemptions and track return on investment for discount campaigns.

**Primary Users:** Revenue Managers, Executive Leadership

**Key Metrics:**
- Total revenue from redeemed offers (USD)
- Average revenue per redemption
- Incremental seats filled via offers
- Revenue by discount tier
- Cost of discounts vs. revenue generated

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Venue name
- Discount percentage
- Offer type
- Date range
- Party size

**Visualization Requirements:**
- KPI cards: Total revenue, average revenue per redemption
- Stacked bar chart: Revenue by venue and discount tier
- Line chart: Revenue trend over time
- Table: Detailed revenue breakdown by offer parameters

**Refresh Frequency:** Hourly

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF

---

### 3.5 REPORT: Offer Performance Analysis

**Business Objective:**  
Analyze which offer characteristics (discount level, party size, timing) drive the highest conversion rates to optimize future campaigns.

**Primary Users:** Marketing Teams, Revenue Managers

**Key Metrics:**
- Conversion rate by discount percentage
- Conversion rate by party size
- Average time to acceptance (minutes)
- Offer effectiveness within TTL (time-to-live) window

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Discount tier (10%, 15%, 20%, 25%)
- Party size (1, 2, 3-4, 5+)
- Target zone
- Venue name
- Time of day

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Column chart: Conversion rate by discount tier
- Scatter plot: Time to acceptance distribution
- Matrix: Conversion rates by discount × party size
- Box plot: Conversion rate variability by venue

**Refresh Frequency:** Daily

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF, PowerPoint

---

### 3.6 REPORT: Low Occupancy Alert Report

**Business Objective:**  
Proactively identify venues experiencing low occupancy and track whether offers are being deployed to address capacity gaps.

**Primary Users:** Operations Managers, Revenue Managers

**Key Metrics:**
- Current occupancy rate (< 45% threshold)
- Available seats
- Number of offers presented in last hour
- Missed opportunity indicator (low occupancy + no offers)

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Venue name
- Time window (15-minute intervals)
- Day part (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
- Alert severity (critical, warning, normal)

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Alert table: Venues with low occupancy (sorted by severity)
- Gauge chart: Current occupancy vs. threshold
- Timeline: Offers deployed during low occupancy periods
- Map view: Venue locations with alert indicators

**Refresh Frequency:** Real-time (updates every 15 minutes)

**Alert Functionality:** Email/SMS notifications when threshold breached

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF

---

### 3.7 REPORT: Guest Journey Tracking

**Business Objective:**  
Understand guest movement paths to identify high-conversion zones and optimize offer placement strategies.

**Primary Users:** Marketing Teams, Operations Managers

**Key Metrics:**
- Common journey paths (origin → destination)
- Journey duration (minutes)
- Conversion correlation (zone → venue)
- Guest segment journey patterns

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Origin zone type
- Destination venue
- Guest segment
- Time of day

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Sankey diagram: Guest flow between zones
- Path analysis chart: Most common journeys
- Bar chart: Journey duration distribution
- Matrix: Zone-to-venue conversion rates

**Refresh Frequency:** Daily

**Export Options:** PDF, PowerPoint

---

### 3.8 REPORT: Inventory Perishability Dashboard

**Business Objective:**  
Quantify unused seating capacity as "perishable inventory" to justify proactive offer deployment and maximize revenue per available seat hour (RevPASH).

**Primary Users:** Revenue Managers, Executive Leadership

**Key Metrics:**
- Minutes until next service period
- Unused capacity hours (available seats × time remaining)
- Table turnover rate
- Revenue per available seat hour (RevPASH)

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Venue name
- Service period (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
- Day of week

**Visualization Requirements:**
- KPI cards: Total unused capacity hours, RevPASH
- Area chart: Capacity utilization over service period
- Bar chart: Turnover rate by venue
- Warning indicators: High unused capacity

**Refresh Frequency:** Every 15 minutes during service periods

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF

---

### 3.9 REPORT: A/B Test Performance Report

**Business Objective:**  
Measure the effectiveness of offer strategies through controlled A/B testing to validate optimization hypotheses.

**Primary Users:** Marketing Teams, Data Analytics Teams

**Key Metrics:**
- Control group revenue
- Test group revenue
- Incremental revenue (test - control)
- Statistical significance (p-value)
- Lift percentage

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Test variant name
- Offer strategy
- Guest segment
- Test duration

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Side-by-side comparison: Control vs. Test metrics
- Waterfall chart: Revenue lift breakdown
- Statistical summary table: Confidence intervals, significance
- Time series: Daily performance comparison

**Refresh Frequency:** Daily (during test period)

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF, PowerPoint

---

### 3.10 REPORT: Discount Optimization Analysis

**Business Objective:**  
Determine the optimal discount level that maximizes net revenue after accounting for discount costs.

**Primary Users:** Revenue Managers, Pricing Analysts

**Key Metrics:**
- Redemption rate by discount tier
- Gross revenue
- Discount cost (revenue × discount %)
- Profit margin (net revenue / gross revenue)
- Cost per acquisition

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Discount tier (10%, 15%, 20%, 25%)
- Party size
- Venue type
- Date range

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Line chart: Redemption rate vs. discount level
- Stacked bar chart: Gross revenue vs. net revenue by tier
- Scatter plot: Profit margin vs. redemption rate
- Table: Cost-benefit analysis by discount tier

**Refresh Frequency:** Daily

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF

---

### 3.11 REPORT: Guest Engagement Metrics

**Business Objective:**  
Track individual guest responsiveness to offers over time to identify engagement fatigue and optimize offer cadence.

**Primary Users:** Marketing Teams, Customer Experience Teams

**Key Metrics:**
- Offer sequence number (1st, 2nd, 3rd+ offer to guest)
- Acceptance rate by offer sequence
- Previous offer response impact
- Fatigue indicators (declining acceptance over time)

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Guest segment
- Cruise day number
- Previous offer count
- Time since last offer

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Line chart: Acceptance rate by offer sequence
- Matrix: Current response vs. previous response
- Cohort analysis: Guest engagement over cruise duration
- Alert indicators: Guests showing fatigue

**Refresh Frequency:** Daily

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF

---

### 3.12 REPORT: Operational Readiness Dashboard

**Business Objective:**  
Monitor system health and data quality to ensure reliable reporting and offer delivery.

**Primary Users:** IT Operations, Data Engineering Teams

**Key Metrics:**
- RTLS signal quality (average confidence score)
- Data latency (minutes)
- Inventory refresh rate (updates per hour)
- Offer delivery success rate

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Source system
- Data feed name
- Time period
- Alert status

**Visualization Requirements:**
- KPI cards: System uptime, data latency, delivery success rate
- Timeline: Data refresh intervals
- Bar chart: Signal quality by zone
- Alert log: System issues and resolutions

**Refresh Frequency:** Real-time (updates every 5 minutes)

**Alert Functionality:** Automated alerts for system degradation

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF

---

### 3.13 REPORT: Venue Yield Scorecard

**Business Objective:**  
Provide a comprehensive performance scorecard for each venue comparing actual performance to potential revenue.

**Primary Users:** Revenue Managers, Venue Operations Managers, Executive Leadership

**Key Metrics:**
- Total covers (baseline + walk-ins + offer-driven)
- Walk-in covers
- Offer-driven covers
- Revenue per cover
- Yield index (actual revenue / potential revenue × 100)

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Venue name
- Service type
- Date range
- Comparison period (vs. previous week/month)

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Scorecard table: Multi-metric performance by venue
- Gauge chart: Yield index (0-100%)
- Stacked bar chart: Covers breakdown (baseline/walk-in/offers)
- Trend line: Yield index over time

**Refresh Frequency:** Daily

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF, PowerPoint

---

**Business Objective:**  
Identify specific time windows with the highest revenue opportunity based on low occupancy, high conversion rates, and strong revenue potential.

**Primary Users:** Revenue Managers, Marketing Teams

**Key Metrics:**
- Average occupancy rate by 30-minute window
- Offers fired per time window
- Conversion rate by time window
- Revenue by time window
- Composite opportunity score

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Time window (30-minute buckets)
- Day of week
- Venue type

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Heat map: Opportunity score by time window and day
- Stacked area chart: Occupancy + conversion + revenue over time
- Top 10 list: Highest opportunity time windows
- Matrix: Time window performance by venue

**Refresh Frequency:** Daily

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF, PowerPoint

---

### 3.16 REPORT: Queue Displacement Analysis

**Business Objective:**  
Measure whether micro-offers reduce walk-in queue sizes or cause displacement issues during high-demand periods.

**Primary Users:** Operations Managers, Guest Experience Teams

**Key Metrics:**
- Average walk-in queue size
- Offers accepted during same time window
- Queue displacement rate (queue reduction / offers accepted)
- Estimated service delay (minutes)

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Venue name
- Time period
- Occupancy level (low, medium, high)

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Line chart: Queue size vs. offers accepted over time
- Scatter plot: Queue size vs. occupancy rate
- Bar chart: Service delay by venue
- Alert indicators: Negative displacement (queue increased)

**Refresh Frequency:** Real-time (every 15 minutes)

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF

---

### 3.17 REPORT: RTLS Coverage Quality Report

**Business Objective:**  
Monitor the quality of guest tracking data to ensure accurate offer targeting and presence analytics.

**Primary Users:** IT Operations, Data Quality Teams

**Key Metrics:**
- Average signal confidence by zone
- Ping frequency (pings per guest)
- Dead zone count (zones with signal confidence < 70%)
- Tracking error rate

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Zone type
- Location ID
- Sensor ID

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Heat map: Signal quality by zone location
- Bar chart: Zones ranked by signal confidence
- Line chart: Signal quality trend over time
- Alert table: Dead zones requiring attention

**Refresh Frequency:** Real-time (every 5 minutes)

**Alert Functionality:** Automated alerts for signal degradation

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF

---

### 3.18 REPORT: Incremental Revenue Attribution

**Business Objective:**  
Quantify the true incremental revenue generated by the Yield Hunter system compared to baseline performance.

**Primary Users:** Executive Leadership, Revenue Managers, Finance Teams

**Key Metrics:**
- Baseline revenue (control period or historical average)
- Revenue with offers enabled
- Incremental revenue (with offers - baseline)
- Incremental covers
- Return on investment (ROI %)

**Dimensions/Filters:**
- Venue name
- Comparison period (week/month/quarter)
- Guest segment

**Visualization Requirements:**
- Waterfall chart: Baseline → Incremental → Total revenue
- Bar chart: Incremental revenue by venue
- KPI cards: Total incremental revenue, ROI %
- Trend line: Incremental revenue over time

**Refresh Frequency:** Weekly

**Export Options:** Excel, PDF, PowerPoint

## 6. USER ACCESS & SECURITY

### 6.1 Role-Based Access
- **Executive Leadership:** All strategic reports (revenue, yield, ROI)
- **Revenue Managers:** All operational and analytical reports
- **Operations Managers:** Venue occupancy, inventory, queue management
- **Marketing Teams:** Offer performance, guest engagement, A/B testing

### 6.2 Data Security
- All guest data must be anonymized in shared reports
- Personally identifiable information (PII) restricted to authorized users only
- Audit logs for sensitive report access

## 7. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

### 7.1 Report Delivery Methods
- **Interactive Dashboards:** Power BI, Tableau, or similar BI platform
- **Scheduled Email Delivery:** PDF/Excel reports sent daily/weekly
- **Mobile Access:** Key dashboards accessible via mobile app
- **API Access:** For custom integrations and data feeds

### 7.2 Performance Requirements
- Real-time dashboards must refresh within 30 seconds
- Batch reports must complete processing within 1 hour of scheduled time
- Historical data retention: Minimum 2 years

### 7.3 Data Quality Standards
- Data accuracy: 99.5% or higher
- Data completeness: No more than 1% missing values
- Timeliness: Real-time data latency < 5 minutes

## 8. APPENDICES

### Appendix A: Glossary of Terms
- **RevPASH:** Revenue Per Available Seat Hour
- **Occupancy Rate:** Percentage of seats occupied at a given time
- **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of offers accepted or redeemed
- **Yield Index:** Ratio of actual revenue to potential revenue
- **Incremental Revenue:** Additional revenue generated beyond baseline

### Appendix B: Success Metrics
- 20% increase in off-peak occupancy rates
- 15% reduction in perishable inventory waste
- 10% overall revenue lift from micro-offers
- 95% offer delivery success rate
